Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/02/2025

SimpleATC ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and any optional profile fields you provide.
  • Usage Data: Scenario attempts, scores, and interactions collected to improve the Service.
  • Device & Log Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access times.
  • Audio Data: Speech recordings processed locally in your browser for scoring (not stored on our servers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service;
  • To personalize training recommendations and track progress;
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts;
  • To monitor usage and improve features and user experience;
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases: (a) performance of a contract, (b) legitimate interests, (c) compliance with legal obligations, and/or (d) your consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in. Analytical cookies may be used to understand usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser, but the Service may not function properly.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited information with third-party processors strictly necessary to operate the Service (e.g., Supabase for authentication, Paddle for payments). These providers are bound by confidentiality and data-processing agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no Internet transmission or storage system is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or transfer your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@simpleatc.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for deletion.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at privacy@simpleatc.com.
